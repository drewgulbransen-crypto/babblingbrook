About the channel

Made in Vancouver.
For real life.

Babbling Brook is a guided meditation channel rooted in the Pacific Northwest — a place that's busy, beautiful, rainy, and occasionally perfect all at once.

These meditations are made for real Vancouver life: the morning commute over the bridge, the long grey January, the Sunday when the mountains finally come out and you just need a moment to be in it.

Short, gentle, and completely free on YouTube. Subscribe and get notified when something new drops.
